Made To Serve

If you’d like God to use you in helping people get closer to Him, then
keep reading.

We love to support people who want to get their faith ‘out of the building.’ We have training and guidance to equip you and provide an environment for God to activate you. If you are curious, then just make contact and we can tell you more. Sometimes people like to do a try-out for one or two sessions, before deciding to commit more fully

W e have different teams and most people go out one Saturday a month, although some like to go more often. Before we go out, we tend to meet for coffee and a catch-up but to pray and seek God as well. There is always a team leader and the leader will appoint a more experienced person alongside anyone with less experience. We don’t see ourselves as experts but just everyday followers of Jesus, who have gained heaps of experience through persistence and have learnt how to develop spiritual boldness and reliance on God. We have seen people come to put their faith in Christ, be baptized and healed also. We have explained the gospel thousands of times and take the conversations as we find them and sometimes all we do is just listen.
