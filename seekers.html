Are You Curious

“.... or even confused about faith, God, Jesus, Christianity? Many of us have been there. Knowledge and information might give us some direction but ultimately, to connect with God, we need a personal encounter, for God to reveal himself to us. Do you have questions? Do you need a God intervention in your circumstances? Would deep peace in your life be a significant thing for you at the moment? Are you open to the possibility of letting the creator reach out to you? Then feel free to reach out to us. Perhaps we can help you on your journey.

As well as being on hand to pray for personal healing (body, mind or soul), or to pray for everyday life issues, we also seek to engage and invite responses to the big ‘life questions’ that people often have deep within them; whether they believe in God or not. We have been active in and around Guildford for over ten years now. We have trained ourselves and are now also training others to engage their faith, in both personal friendship outreach but also in community outreach or town centre evangelism.

We are happy to chat, to see if we can help you on your journey and with any questions that you might have. Feel free to reach out to us via our Contact page (link).
